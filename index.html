Steps for Quilting:

  1. Pick a pattern. Check out the "Stitch Patterns" tab and be sure to include the name of the pattern of your choosing when contacting Keily’s Cozy Quilts.
  2. Thread choices. We carry a variety of solid colored thread. We will select the best color thread to compliment your quilt top. If you have a specific color preference, we’ll do our best to accommodate. We use the same color thread for both the top and backing unless specifically requested.
  3. You may provide your own batting or purchase one of the options we have on hand.
    • $9 a yard - 80/20 Cotton Batting

    All yardage bolts are 93” wide. If your quilt is wider than 85” on both sides, you will need to provide your own batting and ship with your quilt top.

  4. Shipping: We can accommodate any of the following ways to handoff your project and will follow up with details about each in our confirmation email once you’ve filled out our online form.
    • Drop off and pick up from Keily’s Cozy Quilts at our Chandler AZ Location. – Free
    • Ship to Keily’s Cozy Quilts. – Return shipping included on final invoice
